# v1.5.3 Change Summary

- Enforced regime-family priority for `TREND_CONTINUATION`: ORB now has hard priority, while MR is down-ranked and only allowed to override via exceptional counter-trend criteria.
- Added a hard pre-entry payoff gate (points-normalized stop/target/spread/expected slippage) with stricter MR requirements and explicit `BLOCKER_PAYOFF` rejection when geometry is weak.
- Relaxed `OR_TOO_WIDE` only for strong ORB continuation subtypes in `TREND_CONTINUATION` using a wider secondary OR-width limit with mandatory score penalty and diagnostics.
- Delayed management aggression: BE/trailing now require at least two fully closed M5 bars and MFE(R) thresholds before activation (family-aware BE thresholds).
- Expanded decision and management diagnostics to log raw/final family scores, penalties/overrides, payoff distances, blocker/selection reasons, and management guardrail delays/actions.
