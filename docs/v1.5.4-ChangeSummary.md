# v1.5.4 Change Summary

- Hard-blocked ordinary MR in `TREND_CONTINUATION`; MR now only appears through rare exceptional reclaim override with explicit block/override reasons.
- Replaced pessimistic payoff assumptions with realistic expected slippage (`min(max(2.0, spread*0.15), 8.0)`) and corrected ORB/MR payoff thresholds.
- Broadened strong ORB continuation acceptance in OR-width logic to include `ORB_DIRECT_BREAK`, widened secondary allowance to `primary*1.35`, and reduced secondary penalty to `6`.
- Simplified management guardrails and reduced noise: state-change-driven guard logs, delayed BE/trailing thresholds by family, and richer action logs (`bars_since_entry`, `mfe_r`, `oldSL`, `newSL`, reason).
- Expanded diagnostics for one-cycle validation: regime/family/subtype, raw/final scores, MR block/override reasons, payoff distances, OR-width limits/allowance, blocker, and selection reason.
